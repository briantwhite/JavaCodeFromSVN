jsPedigrees

Instructions
  1. Right-click members of the pedigree to edit them
  2. The Edit menu allows un-doing and re-doing changes.
  3. Click "solve" to solve the pedigree (note that this can take a long time if the peidgree is large)
Challenges
  • Build a pedigree and solve it yourself; then click "solve" and see if you got it right
  • Try to build a pedigree that is consistent with a particular mode or modes of inheritance
